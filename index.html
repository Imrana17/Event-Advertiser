Welcome to VintageCon

Join industry leaders, winemakers, sommeliers, and enthusiasts for three days of education, networking, and celebration of the wine industry.

VintageCon brings together the brightest minds in viticulture, oenology, and wine business to share insights, trends, and innovations shaping the future of wine. Whether you're a seasoned professional or new to the industry, you'll find valuable connections and knowledge at this premier event.

About VintageCon

Our History

Founded in 2010, VintageCon has grown from a small gathering of local winemakers to an internationally recognized conference attracting attendees from over 30 countries. Our mission has always been to foster education, innovation, and community within the wine industry.

Each year, we curate a program that balances traditional techniques with cutting-edge advancements, ensuring our attendees stay at the forefront of the industry.

2023 Highlights

  • Keynote presentations from industry pioneers
  • Technical workshops on viticulture and winemaking
  • Business strategy sessions for wine brands
  • Networking events with producers and distributors
  • Grand tasting featuring over 200 wines

Napa Valley Conference Center
