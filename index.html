Welcome to Hackaton 2026

Join top coders, developers, YouTubers, and tech innovators for three days of coding, collaboration, and cutting-edge technology.

Hackaton 2026 brings together the brightest minds in programming, AI, web development, and technology to solve real-world problems, share knowledge, and create innovative solutions. Whether you're a seasoned developer or just starting your coding journey, you'll find inspiration and community at this premier event.

About Hackaton

Our History

Founded in 2015, Hackaton has grown from a small MIT student event to an internationally recognized gathering attracting participants from over 50 countries. Our mission has always been to foster innovation, collaboration, and learning within the tech community.

Each year, we challenge participants to push the boundaries of technology, working together to create solutions that make a difference in the world.
